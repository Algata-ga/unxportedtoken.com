How to setup Trust Wallet for UNXP (UnxPorted Token) ?

Add BNB to your trust Wallet
Convert the BNB to BNB smart chain
Make sure to check the network
Go to DApps > Pancake Swap > Exchange
Select UNXP and then paste the address
