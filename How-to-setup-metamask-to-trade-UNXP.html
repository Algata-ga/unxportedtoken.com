How to setup Metamask wallet for UNXP (UNxPORTED Token) ?

Create MetaMask wallet here
Create a MetaMask Wallet using either a desktop computer or an iOS/Android mobile device.That will allow you to buy, sell, send, and receive UNXP 0x5371E49eFE295A90CaDc5a1aE85a77A2b620da1A
Setup binance smart chain. You can follow this article
Send BNB to your wallet
You can buy BNB bep-20 directly on MetaMask or transfer it to your MetaMask Wallet from exchanges like Coinbase, Binance, etc. Make sure to use the Bep-20 network when transfering BNB.
Connect your wallet to UNxPORTED (UNXP) 18 decimal Token by importing new token from account contract address paste this 0x5371E49eFE295A90CaDc5a1aE85a77A2b620da1A
Access your wallet to UNXP by clicking ‘Connect to a wallet’ and selecting MetaMask.Buy BNB for UNxPORTED token.
You can start swapping as soon as you have BNB available! Press ‘Select a token’ and enter the token address or search for it on the tokens list.
